Terms & Conditions — What is included

Included with every package

  • Design files: Final GUI delivered as PNG/SVG and Roblox-ready assets where applicable.
  • Implementation-ready: UI elements prepared for import into Roblox Studio (frames, buttons, icons).
  • Basic testing: Visual and functional checks in a test place to ensure UI behaves as expected.
  • Support window: 7 days of free support after delivery for minor fixes and guidance.
  • Revisions: Number of revisions depends on the plan (see plan details). Revisions cover layout and visual tweaks, not full redesigns.
  • Delivery time: Estimated delivery is provided per plan and per selected mode; final ETA confirmed after project scope is agreed.

Imported / Animated options

  • Imported: I will import provided assets (icons, sprites) and adapt them to the UI. If assets require conversion, additional time may apply.
  • Animated: Includes basic UI animations (transitions, button hover, simple tweening). Complex timeline animations or cinematics are quoted separately.

What is not included

  • Full game logic, server-side systems, or monetization setup beyond simple UI hooks (these can be quoted separately).
  • Third-party paid assets or licenses — you must provide or purchase them.
  • Extensive reworks that change the original scope — these will require a new quote.

Payment & delivery

  • Payment is accepted in Robux via agreed method (Game Pass, Developer Product, or other mutually agreed flow).
  • Work begins after payment or agreed deposit is received.
  • Final files and delivery are provided after full payment and acceptance of the delivered work.

License & ownership

  • Upon full payment, you receive the right to use the delivered UI in your project(s).
  • I retain the right to showcase the work in my portfolio unless a separate NDA or private agreement is made.

Cancellation & refunds

  • If the project is canceled before work starts, a full refund is possible.
  • If work has already started, refunds are handled pro-rata based on completed milestones and time spent.
